TaraTsek

"Diabetes Life Logging Application"


Monitoring your blood glucose can be burdensome without the appropriate tool. The use of an efficient application can best support daily monitoring of blood glucose and insulin intake. With Tara Tsek, it can provide you various parameters not only limited to Blood glucose values and Insulin dose intake. It incorporates your daily diet and activities as well the monitoring of your mental status. It can be linked in the cloud and can be easily sent to your physician for efficient evaluation.

The use of this app alongside with our blood glucose meter (Glucocard S) can help you attain your goals of quality life.
